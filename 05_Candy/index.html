Candies In America

For this project, we decided to tackle the problem of visualizing the demographic interest in several different categories of candy in the United States. We categorized the candies into Chocolate, Fruit, Other, Gum, Licorice, and Trail Mix.

We decided to group the ages by '6 to 17', '18 to 34', '35 to 59, and '60 and above' (going from right to left). Blue indicates male while pink indicates female. The height of the bar graph was determined by a measurement we called the joy factor. In a short explanation, the higher the bar going up means the more people enjoyed that category, the lower the bar means more people did not enjoy that category and if the bar has little to no height it means it had neutral or no strong feelings about it.

The number shown on the x axis are the total number of people that fit into that section.
